Pocatello, Idaho, USA

Location

Pocatello is a city in southeast Idaho. It's located along Interstate 15 near the Portneuf River and is about 230 miles east of Boise, Idaho.

About

Weather

Almost like every other city in Idaho, Pocatello experiences all four seasons: spring, summer, fall, and winter.

Population

Pocatello has a population of around 57,000 people, and is one of the main cities in southeastern Idaho.

Sports

Some popular sports many high school or college athletes love to play include basketball, football, and track and field.

Things To Do

  • Visit the Idaho Museum of Natural History
  • Walk or bike along the Portneuf Greenway
  • Explore hiking trails in the surrounding mountains
  • Go skiing or snowboarding by nearby resorts like Pebble Creek
